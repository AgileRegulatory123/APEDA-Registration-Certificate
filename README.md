### Step-by-Step Process for APEDA Registration in 2026

**Step 1: Business Setup and IEC Code** Ensure your business is properly registered with GST, PAN, and bank account. The first prerequisite is obtaining an Import Export Code (IEC) from the DGFT portal.

**Step 2: Online Registration on APEDA Portal** Visit the official APEDA website and register on the exporter portal using your IEC details. Fill the online application form with accurate company and product information.

**Step 3: Document Upload** Upload all required documents including IEC copy, GST certificate, PAN card, company registration proof, and list of products to be exported.

**Step 4: Application Review and Payment** After submission, APEDA reviews the application. Pay the registration and membership fees online once your documents are verified.

**Step 5: Approval and Certificate Issuance** Upon successful verification, you receive the digital RCMC. The certificate is usually valid for five years and needs renewal thereafter.
